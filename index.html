Roya Assadollahi

Evil Principles in Selected Middle Persian and Avestan Texts

While some Pahlavi texts focus more on Ahreman, or "Gannāg-Mēnōy," as the ultimate evil principle, Drūz also plays an active role in other Middle Persian works. This project combines comparative and digital humanities methods to examine how these concepts relate to each other, how they differ across the selected corpora, and what these principles may have meant to Mazdayasnian high priests in ninth-century Iran.
